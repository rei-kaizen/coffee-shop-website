About La Bonne Tasse

Our Story

La Bonne Tasse was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a space where people could connect, relax, and enjoy moments of calm in their busy lives.

Founded in 2004 by Denie Rose Bon, our coffee house began as a small corner café with just five tables. Today, we've grown into a beloved community gathering place, but our commitment to quality and personal service remains unchanged.
